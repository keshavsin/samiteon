Data Protection

Samiteon provides a consultative, best practice-based approach to solving business needs and mitigating risk. We have a long, successful history of architecting data protection strategies that focus on backup, business continuity and disaster recovery. Benefit from our architects who have more than 15 years of data protection experience and real-world expertise with industry leading solutions.

Our tools-assisted analysis and engineering-led solutions have delivered measurable results to our customers:

  • Reduced operating costs.
  • Meet compliance and regulatory requirements.
  • Consolidation of multiple backup environments.
  • Emphasis on achieving RTO and RPO.
  • Effective management of data growth.
  • Dependable backup strategies.
  • Cloud integration for archival data.
  • Data center migration.
